Social Engineering Awareness

You receive an email from someone claiming to be your bank, asking you to verify your account details.

Identify the red flags and suggest countermeasures.

Red Flags:

  • Unusual sender address
  • Spelling and grammatical errors
  • Urgent request for personal information
  • Suspicious links or attachments

Countermeasures:

  • Verify the sender's address
  • Contact your bank directly
  • Do not click on suspicious links
  • Report the email to your bank
